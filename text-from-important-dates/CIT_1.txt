 Important Dates   
 11 th  World Congress on Electrical Engineering and Computer Systems and Science (EECSS 2025)  
  August 17 - 19, 2025 | Paris, France   

 The EECSS 2025 Congress is composed of 5 conferences   

 The important dates and deadlines for the 11 th  Congress on Electrical Engineering and Computer Systems and Science (EECSS 2025) are as follows:  
  
 CONGRESS DATES | August 17-19, 2025 
 SUBMISSION  
  DEADLINES | Paper Submission Deadlines | January 23, 2025 
 Notification to Authors | February 28, 2025 
 Final Version of Accepted Submissions | March 20, 2025 
 REGISTRATION  
  DEADLINES | Early-Bird Registration | March 20, 2025 
 Regular Registration* | May 18, 2025 
 Late Registration | After May 18, 2025 
  
 *This is the registration deadline for all authors
