 Dates of Importance  

 Below are the key deadlines and important dates for ICNAAM 2025. Please ensure that all submissions, registrations, and payments are completed by the respective deadlines.  

 Conference Dates | 16-22 September 2025 
 Normal registration deadline*: | 01 July 2025 
 Late registration deadline*: | 08 August 2025 
 Submission of extended abstracts: | 08 August 2025 
 Notification of acceptance: | 09 August 2025 
 Submission of the source files of camera ready extended abstracts to American Institute of Physics (AIP Conference Proceedings): | 25 October 2025 
 Submission of full papers for consideration for publication in the journals: | 30 September 2025 - 31 January 2026 
  
 *All fees must be paid by the deadline, and a bank transaction receipt must be sent to the Secretary of ICNAAM via email, or payment confirmed via Visa, MasterCard, or American Express.
