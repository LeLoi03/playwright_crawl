 Submitting Papers to Our Publications  

 IEEE Transactions on Industry Applications and IEEE Industry Applications Magazine  

 IAS policy requires that papers be presented at approved conferences to become eligible for review and possible publication in IEEE Transactions on Industry Applications or IEEE Industry Applications Magazine. The one exception to this Presentation First Policy is in the case of a call for papers for a special issue of Transactions on Industry Applications. Special Issue papers may be directly submitted, according to the details in the relevant call for papers.  
 For a list of upcoming conferences for opportunities, please visit our conference schedule:  

 View Conference Schedule      

 Open Journal of Industry Applications  

 Authors may submit papers directly to IEEE Open Journal of Industry Applications  . There is no requirement to present preliminary results at a conference prior to submission to the journal.  
 For more details, please review “Information for Authors” on the Open Journal of Industry Applications page  .
