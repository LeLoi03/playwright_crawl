 [Call for Papers] 2025 IEEE 21st International Conference on Automation Science and Engineering (CASE 2025)  

 1.  [Call for Papers] 2025 IEEE 21st International Conference on Automation Science and Engineering (CASE 2025)     
  0  Like             Qiang Huang       Posted 2 days ago  |  view attached       Options Dropdown    
    Dear all,      
 We are excited to announce that submissions for the 2025 IEEE 21st International Conference on Automation Science and Engineering (CASE 2025)   are open!     
 CASE 2025  is one of the flagship conferences of the IEEE Robotics & Automation Society   , providing a primary forum for cross-industry multidisciplinary research in automation.  It will be held from August 17 to August 21, 2025, in Downtown Los Angeles, California, USA  .  Downtown Los Angeles is a dynamic and bustling hub that blends historic charm with modern vibrancy.   
   
 The theme for CASE 2025 is "Secure and Trustworthy Automation." The conference will cover various topics, such as Systems, Automation, Autonomous Systems, Discrete Event Systems, Smart Cities, Robotics, Building Automation, Meso, Micro and Nano-scale, Big Data, Machine Learning, and Information and Communication Technologies. We encourage you to submit proposals for workshops, tutorials, special sessions, regular papers, industry papers, and presentation-only papers related to the conference topics.    
    
 Important deadlines:    
 February 8, 2025:  Special session proposals 
  March 1, 2025   : Regular and special session full papers 
  April 1, 2025   : Workshop/tutorial proposals and industry/work-in-progress paper 
  April 30, 2025   : RA-L, T-RO, RAM, T-ASE presentation-only submission 
     
 To submit your proposals and papers, and for more information, please visit the CASE 2025 website:  https://2025.ieeecase.org/   .    
    
 We look forward to seeing you in Los Angeles, California!    
    
 Best regards,    
    
 Qiang Huang, Professor   
 Epstein Department of Industrial and Systems Engineering   
 University of Southern California    
 General Chair, CASE 2025
