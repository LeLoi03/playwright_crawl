 Abstract Submission  
 Submit extended abstracts of at most four pages using the Easychair link  no later than March 1, 2019, using the template  available on the website of the workshop. The scientific committee will select the presentations for the workshop on the basis of the extended abstracts received and on the available number of slots.  Closed for submission.  Proceedings volume  
 There is the possibility to include accepted submissions in the electronic abstract booklet, but this is not mandatory. Thus, also work in progress or submitted/published elsewhere is welcome. In addition, there will be a special issue of the journal Discrete Applied Mathematics as a post-conference outlet. Scientific committee
