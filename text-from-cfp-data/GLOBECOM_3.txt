 Open Call  
 This publication track is open to all authors' submissions on any subject that is within the scope of the IEEE Communications Magazine. All submissions that are within the scope of the magazine are welcome.  
 Call for Technology Review papers  
 Technology Review articles are longer articles aimed at providing a comprehensive coverage of a given subject technology. Technology review articles are allowed to have up to 14 pages Please see the Manuscript Submission Policy for detailed guidelines about the submission format.  
 Call for Math Corner papers  
 The Math Corner track accepts article submissions on topics that need a more extensive use of mathematical notations. The limitations on math usage are relaxed compared to other ComMag tracks. Please see the Manuscript Submission Policy for detailed guidelines about the submission format.
