 CFP for Int'l Symposium on Problems of Redundancy in Information and Control Systems    
   
 A call for proposals for the XV International Symposium "Problems of Redundancy in Information and Control Systems"   

 Apr 6, 2016    
   
 Anand    
 D.    
 Sarwate    

 We are glad to inform you about coming XV International Symposium "Problems of Redundancy in Information and Control Systems". This conference covers a wide area of aspects of information and communication systems. It will be held in Saint-Petersburg, Russia in 26-29 September 2016.  
 Important dates:   
  Full Paper Submission Deadline:  1 May, 2016   
  Paper Acceptance Notification:  1 June, 2016   
  Final Paper Submission Deadline: 1 July, 2016   
   
  An additional information can be found at official Symposium site  : http://www.redundancy2016.com/
