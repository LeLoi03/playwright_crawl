 FSCD 2025: Call for Papers   
  October 18, 2024, 12:28 pm     
  
 The deadline for submitting a paper to the 10th International Conference on Formal Structures for Computation and Deduction (FSCD'25)  is on 17 February 2025  . FSCD'25 is scheduled to take place in Birmingham, UK, from 14 to 20 July 2025. Please have a look at the Call for Papers  for more information.
